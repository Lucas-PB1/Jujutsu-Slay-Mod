# Jujutsu Kaisen: The Ultimate Sorcerer's Master Grimoire

This document is the definitive, exhaustive technical reference for the "Jujutsu Kaisen: The Cursed Spire" mod.

---

## 1. Fundamentals of Cursed Energy
*   **Reinforcement**: Defensive/Offensive coating.
*   **Flow**: Internal movement of energy.
*   **Divergent Fist**: Delayed impact (Double hit).
*   **Black Flash**: Critical hit to the 2.5th power; requires 0.000001s timing.
*   **RCT (Reverse Cursed Technique)**: Healing and positive energy creation (+).
*   **CTR (Cursed Technique Reversal)**: Inverting an innate technique's effect.

## 2. Character-Specific Innate Techniques

### Satoru Gojo: Limitless
*   **Infinity**: Passive barrier.
*   **Lapse: Blue**: High attraction/Vacuum.
*   **Reversal: Red**: High repulsion/Shockwave.
*   **Hollow Purple**: Matter erasure.
*   **Six Eyes**: 100% efficiency and atom-level perception.

### Ryomen Sukuna: Shrine
*   **Dismantle**: Standard air-slash.
*   **Cleave**: Targeted slash; scales with enemy durability.
*   **Spiderweb**: Destruction of terrain and large areas.
*   **Divine Flame (Fuuga)**: Massive fire arrow.

### Kinji Hakari: Idle Death Gamble
*   **Pachinko Indicators**: Shutter Doors (crushing), Pachinko Balls (projectiles), and Consecutive Effects (re-dos).
*   **Jackpot**: After matching three symbols, Hakari gains infinite cursed energy and automatic RCT (Immortality) for 4 minutes and 11 seconds.
*   **Rough Cursed Energy**: Sandpaper-like texture causing friction damage on every hit.

### Hiromi Higuruma: The Legal System
*   **Judgeman**: All-knowing judge; presents evidence.
*   **Verdicts**: Confiscation (disables techniques), Death Penalty (Executioner's Sword).
*   **The Gavel**: Shape-shifting tool (Hammer, Mallet, Staff, Hook).

### Kokichi Muta: Mechamaru Absolute
*   **Mode: Absolute**: Large mechanical armor powered by years of stored energy.
*   **Ultimate Cannon**: Triple-beam blast (Mouth + Palms).
*   **Pigeon Viola**: Multiple homing missiles.

## 5. Heavenly Restriction (Ten'yo Jujutsu)
*   **Absolute Physicality**: Immense strength, speed, and durability.
*   **Invisibility**: Undetectable by barriers and most sorcerers' senses.
*   **Soul Perception**: Can see and cut the souls of inanimate objects and people.
*   **Notable Users**: Toji Fushiguro, Maki Zenin.

### Physical Feats & Martial Arts
*   **Zero-Step**: Instantaneous movement using pure leg strength.
*   **Air Jump**: Kicking air molecules to move in mid-air.
*   **Soul-Cut Strike**: Precision strike targeting the soul's outline.
*   **Instinctive Counter**: Reflexive dodging and countering.
